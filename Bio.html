About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the HEC Lausanne, University of Lausanne. My research broadly relates to the political economy of minorities in the Indian context. Prior to this, I earned my M.Res. in Economics at the Paris School of Economics. I did my B.A in Economics (with honours) at the University of Delhi. I am supervised by Prof. Ekaterina Zhuravskaya and Prof. Dominic Rohner.
